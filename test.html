Pancha Kosha Yoga

Annamaya Kosha

The physical body or the food sheath. It represents the physical aspect of the human being.

Pranamaya Kosha

The energy or life-force sheath. It governs the flow of vital energy in the body through breath and prana.

Manomaya Kosha

The mental or emotional sheath. It involves thoughts, emotions, and mental processes.

Vijnanamaya Kosha

The wisdom sheath. It represents the intellect and the higher discriminative mind.

Anandamaya Kosha

The bliss sheath. It is the innermost layer, associated with deep peace and joy.
